= Modern WordPress Membership Plugin for Ecommerce, Digital Downloads =

Introducing MemberGlut, the ultimate membership plugin for WordPress. With MemberGlut, users can effortlessly create a variety of subscription plans tailored to their audience's needs. From basic tiers to premium offerings, MemberGlut offers flexibility and ease of customization. Manage memberships with intuitive tools, monitor subscriptions, and provide exclusive content seamlessly. == Installation ==

Installing MemberGlut is just like any other WordPress plugin.

Navigate to your WordPress "Plugins" page, inside of your WordPress dashboard, and follow these instructions:

1. In the search field, enter **MemberGlut**. Click "Search Plugins", or hit Enter.
1. Select **MemberGlut** and click either "Details" or "Install Now".
1. Once installed, click "Activate".
